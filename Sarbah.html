Mensah Sarbah Hall

Sarbah Hall is the first hall to be named after a hero of the nation, Dr. Mensah Sarbah. The hall is undoubtedly the best mixed-hall on campus having won many laurels. The hall boasts of a traditional outfit known as the "OKPO"(Common Sense Rules). It has affiliations with Katanga hall in KNUST and CASFORD of UCC. Members of the hall are unofficially known as The VIKINGS. MOTTO: KNOWLEDGE, HONOUR, AND SERVICE
